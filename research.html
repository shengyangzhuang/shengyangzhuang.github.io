Implementation of the pre-defined behaviors of differential-drive robot

 

Abstract: Intelligent Micromanipulation Robots is a project at the intersection of medicine and engineering. It is about the design and the construction of a micromanipulation robotic system, with a future aim at treating tumor patientshave through specific microinjection of tumor cells. In the system design process, we used zebrafish as a model to test the realization of different functions of the system. In this project, I did some research about the micromanipulation and the image processing of zebrafish based on openCV. For fulfilling my task of image processing of zebrafish, I worked on various jobs, including researching any literature of microscopic methods, gathering references and materials online, and conducting experiments etc..
