Brief Biography

I am a fourth-year bachelor student majoring in Automation at Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) in China. I am now a visiting student at Acoustic Robotics for Life Sciences and Healthcare (ARSL), ETH Zürich in Switzerland, supervised by Prof. Dr. Daniel Ahmed. Prior to ETHz, I was an exchange student at KTH Royal Institute of Technology in Sweden. From 2020-2021, I worked as a research assistant at Research Institute of Intelligent Control and System, directed by Prof. Huijun Gao and Prof. Zhan Li. In the fall of 2021, I worked part-time as a teaching assistant directed by Prof. Jun Li at School of Physics, HIT, responsible for the elective course "Mathematical Principles in College Physics". In the summer of 2021, I was a visiting fellow at International Training Center of UN ILO, working for Global Youth Leadership Academy founded by UN senior official Dr. Liangrong Zu.

In 2021, I was awarded as one of the "Ten Top-notch University Students of Harbin Institute of Technology", which is the highest honour for students at HIT (including Harbin, Weihai and Shenzhen campuses).
